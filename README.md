# FinNews AI – AI-Powered FinTech News Intelligence Platform

## Overview

FinNews AI is an AI-powered FinTech News Intelligence Platform that aggregates real-time financial news and provides intelligent insights using Generative AI. The platform helps users stay informed about financial markets by offering AI-generated news summaries, market sentiment analysis, personalized recommendations, and interactive analytics dashboards.

The project is designed to simplify financial news consumption by transforming lengthy and complex articles into concise, beginner-friendly insights.

---

## Features

### Real-Time Financial News Aggregation

* Fetches real-time financial news from external News APIs.
* Categorizes articles into:

  * Stock Market
  * FinTech
  * Banking
  * Cryptocurrency
  * Global Economy
  * Investments
  * Personal Finance
  * Technology in Finance

### AI-Powered News Summarization

* Generates concise summaries of financial news articles.
* Provides:

  * Key Highlights
  * Market Impact Analysis
  * Investment Insights
  * Beginner-Friendly Explanations
  * Why This News Matters

### Market Sentiment Analysis

* Performs AI-based sentiment analysis for financial articles.
* Displays:

  * Bullish Sentiment
  * Bearish Sentiment
  * Neutral Sentiment
  * Confidence Scores
  * Market Mood Indicators

### Advanced Search and Filtering

### Personalized Recommendations

* Personal Finance

### Interactive Analytics

* News Category Distribution

## How It Works

1. The platform fetches real-time financial news from external APIs.
2. Articles are categorized based on financial domains.
3. Users can search and filter news articles.
4. Gemini AI generates summaries and market insights.
5. Sentiment analysis determines whether the news is Bullish, Bearish, or Neutral.
6. Interactive dashboards visualize financial trends and analytics.
7. Personalized recommendations improve user experience.
